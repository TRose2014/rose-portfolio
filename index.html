A little bit about me.

I currently reside in Seattle, Washington along with my growing family. I decided to enter the tech world after I had my daughter. I always knew I wanted to do something with computers but could never figure out what. Once she was born, I knew I wanted to make a better life for her and decided to do some research. That's when I found HTML/CSS and was instantly hooked!

Once I built my first website I knew that I would eventually obtain the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my new career. I would also have the ability to use my organizational skills and eye for detail to write clean code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.
